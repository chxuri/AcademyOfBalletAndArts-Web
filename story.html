Our Story

The oldest and most prestige ballet school in Northwest Suburbs of Chicago

A Legacy of Dance

The Academy of Ballet & Arts is an educational training school for all our students who wish to learn and experience the joy of dance, and all that it gives to the development of the human body. Academy students reap the rewards of training their mind, body, and spirit, coordinating together in the magnificent language of dance.


The Academy features a unique Early Childhood Dance Program headed by professionally trained and experienced adult instructors. As students progress, they have a world class comprehensive Ballet/Contemporary/Ballroom/National Traditional Folk Dance Programs to set their foundational technical skills for proper development and education for all the Dance Arts. Academy alumni go on to wonderful careers in all the dance arts on Broadway, L.A., and Vegas, and to major world-renowned Companies here in the U.S.
